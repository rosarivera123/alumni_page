Welcome to the ScriptEd Alumni Page!

This page is designed to connect you to the ScriptEd Alumni network, no matter how far you’ve gone. By being a ScriptEd alum, you are now a part of a large network of fellow former-ScriptEd students who are out in the world doing great things! You are your own best network, and you now have access to engage with each other, and build upon your enthusiasm for all things tech and ScriptEd related.

The ScriptEd Alumni Committee seeks to provide learning, networking, and engagement activities for all ScriptEd Alumni to make sure you are given the tools to succeed as you move. Some of the ways you can stay connected to your fellow alumni are through:



  • ScriptEd Alumni Commitee
  • Career Readiness Workshops
  • Monthly Newsletters
  • Scholarship Information
  • Internship and Job Board for ScriptEd Alumni

About the Alumni Relations Coordinator:

I’m Lia and I’m so excited to be working with all of our amazing ScriptEd alumni! I am here to support ScriptEd seniors and alumni with finding resources to help them stay on track with their post-high school endeavors. Students usually turn to me for help with finding programs to attend after high school, internships, and scholarships. Please reach out to me at lia@scripted.org if you have any questions or concerns!
